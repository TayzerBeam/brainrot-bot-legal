Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Brainrot Translator Discord Bot ("the Bot") handles your information.

2. Data Collection

The Bot does not store, retain, or share any personal data or message content. All processing is performed in real-time and no data is saved.

3. Message Processing

Messages sent to the Bot are processed only for the purpose of generating a response. No logs or records of messages are kept.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot may use third-party AI services (such as Hugging Face) to process messages. These services may have their own privacy policies. The Bot does not control or store data sent to these services.

5. Discord Platform

Use of the Bot is subject to Discord's own privacy policy and terms of service.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot administrator via Discord.
